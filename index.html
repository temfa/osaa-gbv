In Nigeria, as with other parts of the world, children are at risk from violence, abuse, exploitation, and neglect. Global actions and instruments have been developed to curb the violation of the rights of children and to sensitise the public on the safeguarding of children, with different countries at various levels of implementation. Specific to Nigeria, commercial sexual exploitation and trafficking, child labor, and child marriage are problems in many parts of the country. At-risk children and adolescents need their rights enforced if they are to be protected from harm and all forms of violence, as well as to ensure that they develop to their full potential. This e-learning platform, therefore, provides opportunity for individuals, officials and caregivers who work with, and have the mandate to protect children, to strengthen their knowledge and skills in child-protection and safeguarding.
